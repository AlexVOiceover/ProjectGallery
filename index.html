Bench PSU

The case is modular, so every wall can be printed flat on the bed, this way the finish is very smooth. If the case needs to be modified (adding a new conextion type) it can be printed just the affected face instead of the whole case.

When working on electronics it is very important to have an adjustable power supply. This one can provide up to 50V and 5A, what is plenty for most of my projects, even running motors from big quadcopters.

All the STL files will be published in Thingiverse as well as the original parametric files.

The case combines structural parts (printed in PLA) as well as some other elastic (printed in TPU). The case was designed to be printed in a regular 220x200 bed.

Project based on KRALYN3D's power supply.
